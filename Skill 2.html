ABOUT MY GAME. Win with character in Apex Legends, a free-to-play heroic shooter* where legendary characters with powerful abilities battle for glory and riches on the far reaches of the Frontier. Discover a growing collection of diverse lore, deep tactical team battles, and daring innovations that go far beyond "battle royale," all in a gritty world where anything is possible. Meet the new word in the heroic shooter genre. KEY FEATURES Legendary Characters. - Discover a growing collection of powerful legends, each with their own unique characters, skills and unique advantages. It's easy to learn the fundamentals of each legend, but learning how to play as one is a much tougher task. Build your team. - Pick your own Legend and use their skills in combination with other players to create an invincible squad. Strategic team play. - Don't falter when you're fighting a "battle royal" in a huge soaring city or compact arena. Master your legend's unique abilities and coordinate with allies to pick up new tactics and powerful combinations. Innovative Combat. - Use an ever-growing arsenal of powerful weapons and equipment. Think on the move and learn the rhythm of each gun to get the most out of your weapons. Also, don't forget to try new things in timed modes and be ready for the mountain of new products that each season brings. Living Universe. - Apex Legends is set in an exciting universe where things are constantly happening, maps change every season, and more legends enter the fray. Embark on an epic adventure and make your name in Apex Games history with an array of unique costumes!
